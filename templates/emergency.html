What To Do In Case of Cyber Fraud

Follow these immediate steps to protect yourself

Immediate Actions (0-1 Hour)

  1. 1 Stay calm. Do NOT panic - quick but thoughtful action is key.
  2. 2 Note down all details: scammer's number, bank account, transaction ID, screenshots.
  3. 3 Call your bank immediately to freeze the account. Use the 24/7 helpline.
  4. 4 Change all passwords: email, banking, social media.
  5. 5 Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.

Report Within 24 Hours

  1. 1 File complaint at cybercrime.gov.in or visit nearest police station.
  2. 2 Call 1930 to report the transaction and freeze funds if possible.
  3. 3 Visit your bank branch with all transaction documents.
  4. 4 File FIR at local police station if amount is significant.
  5. 5 Report to your telecom provider if the fraud was via call/SMS.

What NOT To Do

Avoid these common mistakes that can make things worse

Don't Panic Share

Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone claiming to be from your bank or a "service provider."

Don't Click Unknown Links

Avoid clicking links in unsolicited messages, even if they look legitimate. Always verify the source.

Don't Transfer Money "To Save It"

Beware of calls asking you to transfer money to a "safe account." This is always a scam.

Don't Delete Evidence

Keep all screenshots, messages, and transaction records. They are crucial for investigation.

Don't Pay "Processing Fees"

Legitimate recovery services don't ask for upfront fees. Be skeptical of such offers.

Don't Ignore It

Even if you lost a small amount, report it. It helps build cases against scammers.

Quick Safety Tips

Simple habits that protect you from most cyber threats

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for each account. Enable 2FA whenever possible.

Verify URLs

Always check the URL before entering sensitive information. Look for HTTPS and correct spelling.

Privacy Settings

Review and update privacy settings on social media regularly.

App Safety

Download apps only from official stores. Check reviews and permissions.
