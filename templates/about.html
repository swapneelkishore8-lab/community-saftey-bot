Our Mission

In an increasingly digital world, misinformation and cybercrime pose significant threats to individuals and communities. The Community Safety Bot was created to bridge the gap between technological threats and public awareness.

Our AI-powered platform provides accessible, reliable, and instant guidance to help people identify potential threats, report cybercrime, and make informed decisions about their digital safety.

We believe that everyone deserves to feel safe online, and we're committed to democratizing cyber safety knowledge for all.

What We Do

Comprehensive protection for your digital life

Misinformation Detection

AI-powered analysis of messages to identify potential misinformation, fake news, and suspicious content before it spreads.

Cybercrime Support

Guidance on reporting cybercrime, accessing helplines, and taking immediate action if you become a victim.

Safety Education

Empowering users with knowledge about online threats, their rights, and preventive measures.

Fraud Prevention

Protecting users from banking fraud, OTP scams, phishing attacks, and other financial cybercrimes.
